Wonder Week!

September Edition

One week out of each month, llamas take a break from work. During this time, the llamas hold events and gain special abilities.

Family Reunion

Monday

Family comes first, they say! The alpacas and the llamas come together to just hang out and catch up.

Leaf-Catching Contest

Tuesday

Only one day in September do the leaves fall from the trees. Whichever llama catches the most leaves receive an extra week off of work!

Fried Chicken Magic

Wednesday

The local fried chicken restaurant, Chick'n, dips its chicken in special rainbow dust. It gives the llamas long luxurious rainbow hair.

Quidditch Free-Play

Thursday

A Harry Potter movie marathon is played on 4 jumbotrons built in Llama-ville's sports stadium where they set up a quidditch field. The audience can watch the game and the movie simultaneously!

Saturn Surfing

Friday

A llama's favorite activity is to go surfing. Even the most vicious waves in Hawaii don't satisfy their wild side. It's not a big deal because their special surfboard can fly them to Saturn faster than the speed of light.

Glowing in the Dark

Saturday

They call this a blessing and curse. This happens all week long, so by the third day, the novelty wears off and it's hard for them to sleep, but they save a ton on their Con Ed bill.

Fireworks Finale

Sunday

On the 4th of July, the American Llama Group sponsors a fireworks show and caters over 4000 tons of grass, the gluten-free variety. They gather in a secluded area and watch the fireworks for a last celebration.
